The problem

The amount of sweat/sodium lost can vary up to 15 times between seemingly similar individuals. Gatorade sports science institute has been performing scientifically-based Sweat Testing to determine athletes' specific hydration needs. Sweat testing is done using special equipment and qualified professionals. It required athlete fluid intake and weight measuring and a lab environment to process results.

Current experience audit & shadowing

The Patch

Single use patch worn on the inner forearm that measures sweat rate and sodium composition. The companion mobile app utilizes image processing to read patch and deliver sweat rate, sodium composition, and personalized sports fuel recommendations
